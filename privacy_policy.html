Privacy Policy for Spark Search: Word Puzzle

Effective Date: April 27, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Smartcore Labs ("we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use the Spark Search: Word Puzzle mobile application (the "App").

Spark Search: Word Puzzle is a server-free and account-free application. We do not require or allow you to create an account, and we do not store your game data on any cloud server.

Spark Search: Word Puzzle is designed to keep gameplay data on your device. We do not operate a custom backend for game saves, and we do not provide cloud save in the current version.

1. Information We Handle

1.1 Data Stored Locally on Your Device

Most gameplay and preference data is stored locally on your device using local app storage. This may include:

  • Player name and player identifier
  • Unlocked levels and level records
  • Completion times and scores
  • In-game balances such as coins and hints
  • Daily login and streak state
  • Settings and preferences, including audio, haptics, and display preferences

1.2 Support and Feedback

If you contact us for support or feedback, we may receive your email address and any information you include in your message.

Support email: smartscanai.contact@gmail.com

2. How We Use Information

We use information handled by the App to:

  • Provide core gameplay functionality
  • Save progress, settings, and personal records on your device
  • Manage in-game items such as coins and hints
  • Process and restore eligible in-app purchases through the relevant app store
  • Show ads, including rewarded ads, banner ads, and interstitial ads
  • Provide ad privacy choices where required

3. Third-Party Services

3.1 Advertising

The App uses Google Mobile Ads / AdMob for advertising features. AdMob and its partners may process device- or ad-related information, such as advertising identifiers, diagnostics, or other information needed to deliver and measure ads.

Google Privacy & Terms: https://policies.google.com/privacy

3.2 In-App Purchases

Payments are processed by the Apple App Store or Google Play Store. We do not collect or store your payment card details ourselves.

  • Apple Privacy Policy: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/
  • Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

4. Data Retention and Deletion

  • Local gameplay data remains on your device until you remove the App or clear app data.
  • Uninstalling the App will generally delete locally stored progress and settings from that device.
  • Data processed by third parties is governed by their own retention practices.

5. Children's Privacy

Spark Search: Word Puzzle is not specifically directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children through our own systems. Because the App contains advertising and in-app purchases, you should ensure your store age rating and audience settings remain consistent.
